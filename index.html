After all six members of the group took the MBTI personality test and the learning style test, the following results were obtained: three members of the group were all ENFJ-A personalities, while the remaining three were ESFJ-A, ENFJ-A, and ISTP-T types. In the Learning Style test, three of the group tended to be visual learners while the remaining three styles were auditory learning style, theoretical learning style, and kinesthetic learning style.


The two pie charts provided show that the ENFJ-A personality type, as well as the visual learning style, made up half of the group. Although half of the team members were of the same personality type, this does not indicate that the group members with more identical types of personality as well as learning styles could not help the team succeed.

Through the MBTI personality test, we can learn and understand separately that group members help to:
1. Bring out individual strengths.
2. Avoid and resolve conflicts.
3. Work together more effectively.
4. Be able to self-understand and understand others, thus reducing the stress of group work.
5. Identify gaps in the group and develop effective plans to address them.

Four of our group members are intuitive ENFJ types, and ENFJ personalities are generally idealistic, preferring to envision an ideal world and wanting to work toward creating that vision. I don't think excessive idealism is good for group work because it can lead to group members feeling good about themselves and sometimes being overly optimistic when faced with a problem and thus unable to complete a task with care. But they can also be genuinely motivated by what they believe to be real goals and sensitive to the emotional needs of others. These types of personalities are good at building on the talents of others because they have a strong desire for harmony and are good at resolving conflicts.

In real group work, I think these types of personalities are good at motivating the group and do not make group work difficult to coordinate because they are generally outgoing and good at resolving conflicts that arise from a lack of authentic communication in the group. They can also lead our team to explore our group learning style.

Another member of our group is an ESFJ with a sensing judgment type and an ISTP with an intuitive feeling type. The ESFJ type is more of a consul and excels at analyzing options and developing realistic plans because they are more focused on traditional methods and believe that these traditional methods provide security, stability, and a sense of belonging to each plan.

ISTP personalities are intuitive perception types who can observe their environment keenly and can improvise solutions to crises that the group may face. By having an ISTP type in the group, we can avoid excessive optimism about the future due to too many ENFJ-type idealists.

So I think our group is balanced and diverse. Idealists and consuls ensure harmony and perfect coordination among the group members, while intuitive types of teammates are keen to identify problems with the plan and provide the most effective solutions.

    Project Description

    Welcome to our Project Description!


    Overview

    • Topic

      • Nowadays, more and more people pay more attention to what they wear, but with the rapidly developing society, people can allocate very little time for comfortable research on what to wear, so we designed the “Storage” app to solve this problem. The app will work with the checkroom we designed in assignment 2. Users can find the app in the app store and use it by linking the Bluetooth of the fitting mirror in the checkroom.

      The app will collect the user's clothing data and provide the user with suitable outfit suggestions and fashion news that the user will like through big data, and will help the user to automatically sort the clothing categories, reducing the amount of time the user spends on dressing and finding clothes. In addition to the core functions, the app also comes with the function of managing the lights and environment of the smart checkroom, users can do remote control, not only to save energy but also to create a comfortable changing environment. the app also comes with security control to prevent any accidents for the elderly and children at home, creating a new fitting experience for users.

    • Motivation

      • When we discussed how to upgrade the checkroom together, we coincidentally thought of app development. Nowadays, more people choose to do everything on their cell phones, and with just two easy clicks, you can do anything you want, which is the convenience that the Internet brings to people. In addition, we combine our experience to design this app, I believe everyone has had the time when they cannot think of what to wear out, if this time there is a person who understands us to provide us with useful advice, this is how happy a thing. Not only that, when you are anxious to find the T-shirts, you bought last year but have no idea where you put them, have you ever longed for an app that can help you find them. Our app is the starting point to solve people's most basic problems and carry the basic control function of the smart home. If we can continue to work on this project, we will give users a perfect changing experience in cloakroom.

    • Landscape

      • Look today, many smart home apps are appearing in the market, such as Amazon's “Alexa”, Samsung's “smart things” and “AppleHome”, but none of the smart home apps are focused on the cloakroom, a place with some personal privacy. Most of the smart home apps on the market are tied to their own devices, but our storage can accept different smart devices and simply connect them to the app to control these devices, which I think is an innovation that should not be underestimated.

    Detailed Description

    • Aims

      • The aim of our project is to design an intelligent cloakroom and a mobile phone intelligent application to help users interact with the cloakroom. We hope to integrate innovative technology into modern life, and use technology, humanized design and intimate functions to help people store and manage clothes. In addition, we hope that the cloakroom can not only facilitate people's life, but also bring reasonable clothing planning for people who need to dress up, so that the life of users will become more interesting and colorful (Moen 2011).

      • Goals

        • In order to help us achieve the intelligent cloakroom and the design of mobile phone applications, we have set some small goals to promote the development of our project:

        1. Understand people's needs for clothing today:
          Now people's living standard is higher and higher, people's pursuit is also higher and higher, not only that, people's requirements for their own dress is also increasing, people pay more attention to dress (BeTALIST 2016). Therefore, we need to understand today's people's needs for clothing, and introduce their needs into the design of our intelligent cloakroom and wardrobe, which can not only meet people's needs, but also help our design become more reasonable.
        2. Study the structural design of the wardrobe and the whole cloakroom:
          The internal structure design of the cloakroom and the wardrobe is very important for us, because its structure design will affect the clothing arrangement of the intelligent wardrobe and the user's experience of using the cloakroom. Intelligent wardrobe has high requirements for the internal space, its structural design should not affect the intelligent operation, so we need to reasonably arrange the internal space and make structural design changes according to the actual situation (Casto 2015). In addition, in order to improve the user experience of the whole smart cloakroom, we need to design a more comfortable cloakroom structure to improve the user efficiency.
        3. Study the working and operating mode of intelligent cloakroom:
          In order to improve the user experience of the intelligent cloakroom, we may need to refer to some of the intelligent cloakrooms that have been put into use in the market, understand their current work and operation mode, learn their strengths, and find some aspects that we can improve the design, and then we can think about the design direction of our mobile application according to this operation mode.
        4. Research on the interaction of mobile phone application with users and intelligent cloakroom:
          Finally, we need to think about the interaction design of mobile applications. In today's application market, there are many intelligent application management software, such as smart TV, air conditioning, and even curtains, which have corresponding mobile intelligent applications to control. For users of intelligent cloakroom, they need to control the cloakroom intelligently, because in this way, they can arrange the operation of sorting clothes by themselves. This design is undoubtedly more humanized and efficient.

        As for our goals, the two most important aspects are the research on people's wearing needs and the structural design of intelligent cloakroom and wardrobe, because these two are the most basic aspects of our project and the foundation of our project. Intelligent cloakroom is for the majority of users, and the needs of users have a guiding role for our design direction. Without the foreshadowing of these two aspects, our subsequent project plans can’t be carried out.
